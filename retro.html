The War Within — Big Picture
10th expansion — launched Aug 26, 2024
First chapter of the Worldsoul Saga trilogy (TWW → Midnight → Last Titan, all before 2030)
Announced BlizzCon Nov 2023 — Metzen back — "We ain't screwin' around"
First full expansion cycle under Microsoft ownership
Metacritic 85 — 97% recommended — IGN 9/10 — one of the best-reviewed expansions ever
Setting: Khaz Algar, subterranean continent. Level 70→80
Major Features
Delves — 1-4 player solo-friendly PvE. Brann companion with his own talent tree. Role-agnostic
Warbands — account-wide everything. Shared bank, rep, achievements. Pre-patch Jul 23
Hero Talents — 11-node sub-spec trees (71-80). Mountain Thane, Templar, Dark Ranger, etc.
Earthen — titan-forged stone dwarves. Both factions, 9 classes. Unlock after campaign
Skyriding — dragonriding on ALL mounts, ALL zones
Cross-Realm Guilds — different servers, same guild. Mythic raiding day one
Arachnophobia Mode — spiders become crabs. Great accessibility call
Zones & Story
Isle of Dorn — surface start. Earthen homeland. Hub: Dornogal
Ringing Deeps — underground. Titan machines. Kobold allies
Hallowfall — Beledar crystal (Light↔Void). Arathi people, Faerin Lothar. Best zone of the expansion
Azj-Kahet — nerubian kingdom. Queen Ansurek. Dark and gloomy
Villain: Xal'atath — Harbinger of the Void. Destroys Dalaran, seemingly kills Khadgar
Anduin reconnects with the Light. Alleria hunts Xal'atath throughout
Arc goes: Ansurek defeated → Gallywix arc → Ethereals steal Dark Heart → K'aresh → Dimensius killed
Khadgar found alive inside the Dark Heart — now wheelchair-bound
Season 1 — Nerub'ar Palace
Starts Sep 10, 2024 — Mythic+M raid Sep 17
8 bosses — final boss Queen Ansurek
World First: Liquid, Sep 29, 404 pulls on Ansurek. Echo 2nd, Method 3rd
M+ pool: Ara-Kara, City of Threads, Stonevault, Dawnbreaker + Mists, Necrotic Wake, Siege of Boralus, Grim Batol
New affix system: Xal'atath's Bargain (rotating weekly)
20th Anniversary
Oct 22 – Jan 6 (Patch 11.0.5) — longest anniversary event ever
Hub outside Caverns of Time — Bronze Dragonflight phased the celebration into reality
BRD reimagined as 10-15 player raid. Codex of Chromie (AQ scenario). Classic Timewalking. Korrak's Revenge
HD Tier 2 sets for all 13 classes — huge nostalgia
Came back as 21st Anniversary (Nov 2025) — now annual, recurring. Same hub, slightly scaled back
It was the 20th, not the 25th — WoW launched Nov 2004
Season 2 — Liberation of Undermine
Patch 11.1 Feb 25 — Season 2 starts Mar 4, 2025
Undermine — goblin city. 4 cartels, weekly alignment. Gallywix vs Gazlowe revolution
8 bosses — final boss Chrome King Gallywix (massive mech suit)
WFR: Liquid again — Day 12, only 100 pulls on Gallywix. Mid-bosses harder (Stix 116, Mug'Zee 149)
D.R.I.V.E. — customizable kart mount (G-99 Breakneck). Up to 670% speed. Drifting, turbo, racing courses
Operation: Floodgate — first brand-new dungeon ever to go straight into M+ rotation
M+ also had MOTHERLODE!!, Theater of Pain, Mechagon Workshop
Season 3 — Manaforge Omega
Patch 11.2 Aug 5 — Season 3 starts Aug 12, 2025
K'aresh — shattered Ethereal homeworld (from BC Netherstorm lore). Destroyed by Dimensius
Reshii Wraps (artifact cloak) — transforms into ethereal form. "Phase Diving" for hidden content
8 bosses — optional boss (Soul Hunters, first since Ulduar) — final boss Dimensius (largest raid boss ever)
New dungeon: Eco-Dome Al'dani. M+ also had Tazavesh (both wings), Halls of Atonement
Patch 11.2.7 Dec 2, 2025 — Player Housing (Midnight pre-purchasers). Brawler's Guild returns
Midnight expansion revealed at Gamescom Aug 2025
Siren Isle
Patch 11.0.7 — Dec 17, 2024. Small storm-wrecked island, weekly rotating enemies
Cyrce's Circlet — upgradeable ring (not belt), ilvl 639→658. Gem sockets, weekly boons
Reception: lackluster. Small, barren, ghost town fast. Forums: "aggressively lame"
Some defended it — ring was decent catch-up, secrets were fun. Filler content between seasons
The D.I.S.C. Belt
Patch 11.1.7 — Jun 2025. D.I.S.C. = Durable Information Securing Container
Upgradeable waist from Overcharged Delves. ilvl 691→701. Swappable active effects (9 powers)
Best power: Charged Bolts — so strong it was mandatory. Later nerfed 62%
The real problem: same slot as Engineering Nitro Boosts (rocket boots)
Belt has a "Use" effect → can't tinker Nitro Boosts onto it. Can't swap belts in M+
So much power you HAD to use it — but losing rocket boots felt terrible for mobility
Turned Delves from fun to a chore. Every belt drop from other content felt wasted
Resolution: Bolts nerfed, then in Season 3 Overcharged Delves removed. Raid belts could replace it. Relief
I took a hard stance against it — same slot as rocket boots for getting around
The Addon Apocalypse
Midnight pre-patch Jan 20, 2026. Blizzard called it "addon disarmament"
Ion: possibly "the single largest change an expansion has ever made"
Long telegraphed: hints Apr 2025 → Alpha Oct → Ion blog Nov 19 → pre-patch Jan 20
What changed: combat data became "secret values" — addons can display but can't process
CAN: resize, re-skin, reposition UI frames. Visual customization only
CANNOT: conditional logic, automated rotations, enemy CD tracking, addon-to-addon comms in instances
WeakAuras — dead for retail. Core functionality impossible. Continues in Classic only
ElvUI — paused, then reversed. Survived with reduced features after Blizzard loosened restrictions
Also dead: Healbot, GTFO, Raider.IO, World Quest Tracker, Mythic Dungeon Tools
DBM, BigWigs, Details — survived but now just skins over Blizzard's built-in systems
Only 1,158 addons compatible by pre-patch week
Performance: WeakAuras was #1 CPU hog — 50-70 FPS loss documented, 18% CPU in raids
Addons estimated to cost 20-30% performance in heavy combat. Performance went up without them
I never really used them so didn't care — but massive deal for most players
Talent reworks came with pre-patch too: level 80→90, 10 new points, Apex Talents
New spec: Devourer Demon Hunter. Classes pruned — devs admitted they designed around addon use
Overlap area — technically Midnight pre-patch, not TWW, but the transition point
Microsoft & Activision
Acquisition completed Oct 13, 2023 — $75.4 billion, largest gaming deal ever
Kotick out Dec 2023. Cloud rights sold to Ubisoft for UK regulatory approval
TWW was already deep in development — launched 10 months post-acquisition
Microsoft → Activision → Blizzard → WoW. Decisions trickle down
Layoffs
Jan 2024 — 1,900 cut. Mike Ybarra + Allen Adham (co-founder) gone. Survival game killed
Sep 2024 — 650 more (corporate/support)
Jul 2025 — 9,000+ Microsoft-wide. Warcraft Rumble dev stops. ~100 Blizzard hit. Perfect Dark cancelled
Total: roughly 4,150+ gaming jobs cut in ~18 months
Phil Spencer Retires
Feb 20, 2026 — memo leaked, confirmed on X. Official retirement Feb 23
38 years at Microsoft (intern 1988). Xbox since 2001. Head of Xbox 2014. CEO MS Gaming 2022
Advisory role through summer to help transition
Sarah Bond (Xbox President) also resigned same day
Jul 2025 Xbox comms denied retirement rumors — timetable apparently changed
Asha Sharma — New CEO
Named CEO of Microsoft Gaming Feb 20, 2026
Born 1989. No gaming industry experience — AI/tech background. Reports to Nadella
Career: S.C. Johnson → Microsoft marketing → Porch Group → VP at Meta (Messenger) → COO Instacart → Microsoft CoreAI → here
Memo: "We will not chase short-term efficiency or flood our ecosystem with soulless AI slop"
Matt Booty promoted to EVP & Chief Content Officer under her
An AI exec with no gaming experience now sits above all of Blizzard — worth watching
